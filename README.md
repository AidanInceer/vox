## ‚ùì FAQ

<details>
<summary><strong>Why isn't my voice being transcribed?</strong></summary>

- Check your microphone is set as the default recording device in Windows
- Speak clearly and avoid background noise
- Ensure the recording indicator appears when you press the hotkey
- Try increasing microphone volume in Windows settings

</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>Can I change the hotkey?</strong></summary>

Yes! Open Vox ‚Üí Settings tab ‚Üí Hotkey Configuration. Click the input field and press your desired key combination.

</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>Does this work with languages other than English?</strong></summary>

Currently optimized for English. Multi-language support is planned for a future release.

</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>How accurate is the transcription?</strong></summary>

95%+ accuracy for clear speech in quiet environments. Accuracy depends on:

- Microphone quality
- Background noise
- Speech clarity and pace
- Accent (trained on diverse English accents)

</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>Can I use this offline?</strong></summary>

Yes! After the first launch downloads AI models (~150MB), everything runs 100% offline.

</details>
